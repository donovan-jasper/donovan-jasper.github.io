<span style="font-size: 1.5em;">From The Nutcracker </span>

In 2024, I performed the Grand Pas de Deux from Tchaikovsky's *The Nutcracker* again. This time, a friend got injured, and I was called to fill in with less than a week to rehearse towards the end of a heavy quarter. The skill of my dance partner, Madi Yeung, greatly contributed to making the performance a possibility. Joining during Tech Week was intense but a lot of fun. 

The Grand Pas de Deux is a highlight of *The Nutcracker*, showcasing a beautiful and technically challenging dance between the Sugar Plum Fairy and her Cavalier.
